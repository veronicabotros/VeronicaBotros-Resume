I specifically worked with tomatoes and cow pea which are two very important vegetables in the world. I went through many ups and downs, faced many issues and even failures, however, I did not give up and continued working on my goal and succeeded. Besides working in a lab during my college career, I have also been volunteering at several different animal hospitals and shelters and have fallen in love with this career. I have been able to see many different surgeries and learn the anatomy of cats and animals without a textbook. I have been able to learn about the different breads of these animals in a similar way and see how each one differs in both appearances as well as personalities. I love animals and am passionate about helping them feel better. Specifically, I have worked at one animal hospital since highschool and have become very familiar with the obstacles that this career requires as well as the daily routine. I have spent many hours doing hands on volunteer work with larger animals such as horses. I truly believe that becoming a veternarian is my passion and the career for me.
